Safety Opening Prevention Device

Unintended opening and accidental access are major causes of industrial accidents. COREON presents a safety opening prevention device designed to structurally block unintended operations in industrial and high-risk environments.

Purpose of the Device

This device is designed to prevent accidental opening, human error, and unauthorized access through a mechanical structure rather than external locks.
