COREON – Frequently Asked Questions

Is COREON a construction contractor?

No. COREON focuses on project management (PM/CM) and structural risk control, not direct construction execution.

Are COREON’s patents currently registered?

Multiple patents have been filed and are currently under examination, with registration expected.

Is collaboration possible before patent registration?

Yes. Concept-level discussions and NDA-based consultations are possible prior to registration.

How is COREON different from large EPCM companies?

COREON operates as an independent management structure designer, focusing on flexibility and on-site risk reduction.

Which industries can apply COREON’s frameworks?

Construction, industrial facilities, infrastructure, and defense-related environments.

How does licensing work?

Licensing structures are discussed after patent registration. Currently, COREON supports preliminary consultations.
